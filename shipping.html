Shipping Policy

1. Order Processing Time

We process all orders within 1-2 business days, excluding Sundays and public holidays. Orders placed after 2 PM IST are processed on the next working day. Once shipped, a confirmation email or SMS with tracking details will be sent to you.

2. Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on your delivery location and selected shipping method. Delivery timelines vary by city and state:

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 3-7 business days ₹49 - ₹99 (varies by pin code)
Express Delivery 1-3 business days ₹149
Same-Day/Next-Day (Selected Cities) Within 24 hours ₹199

3. Free Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all orders above ₹999. This offer is valid across most pin codes in India.

4. Delivery Coverage

We currently deliver to over 20,000+ pin codes across India through trusted courier partners. You can check availability by entering your pin code at checkout.

5. Cash on Delivery (COD)

COD is available for orders up to ₹5,000. A small COD fee of ₹30 may apply. We recommend prepaid orders for faster processing and contactless delivery.

6. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also track your order anytime from the My Account section.

7. Shipping Delays

While we strive for timely delivery, delays may occur due to weather, festivals, logistical issues, or incorrect addresses. Our support team will keep you informed and assist in any way possible.

8. Wrong or Damaged Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product, please contact us within 7 days of delivery at support@pawpartners.com. Share photos and your order ID, and we'll resolve the issue promptly.

9. Contact Us

Still have questions? Reach out to our customer support team at support@pawpartners.com or call us at +91-XXXXXXXXXX between 9 AM - 5 PM (Mon-Sun).
