Shipping Policy

1. Order Processing Time

We process orders within 1-2 business days, excluding weekends and holidays. Orders placed after 2 PM are processed the next business day. A confirmation email with tracking details will be sent once your order ships.

2. Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout. Estimated delivery times:

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 3-7 business days $5.99
Expedited Shipping 2-3 business days $9.99
Overnight Shipping 1-2 business days $19.99

3. Free Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50 within the continental US.

4. International Shipping

We ship internationally to select countries. Rates are calculated at checkout. Customers are responsible for customs fees, taxes, or import duties.

5. Order Tracking

After shipping, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order on the My Account page.

6. Shipping Delays

We are not liable for delays due to carriers, weather, or incorrect addresses. We'll assist where possible to resolve issues.

7. Wrong or Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 7 days at support@yourstore.com and we'll help you as quickly as possible.

8. Contact Us

Questions? Contact our support team at support@yourstore.com.
